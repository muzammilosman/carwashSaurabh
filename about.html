WHO WE ARE


At Aston Car wash we do not use water to clean vechiles we spray a solution and wipe off the dirt and use vacuum cleaner for sucking dirt and dust inside the car perfect for urban dwellers because we deliver our service right at your doorstep. Once the client fills in his requirements, we start cleaning their car at their preferred location at their convenient time and charges is as per the rate chart available on the website. The spray which we use safely lifts away dirt and Grim while leaving behind a protective layer of wax coating. In little as thirty minutes this new formula of washing can morph a drab and dirty appearance into a shiny clean and shiny car without a drop of water.

Its special lubricants and cleaning agents safely loosen and then gently remove the dirt. Followed with a quick wipe from a clean and dry towel and the surface is left with a beautiful and glossy finish. Wash and wax can safely clean your vehicle having any glossy paint or clear coal finish. The waterless formula ensures that your vehicle remains clean and impressive, leaving a shiny layer of wax coating to prevent loose dust from sticking again while the vehicle is on the move.
